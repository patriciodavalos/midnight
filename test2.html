The Hubs manufacturing network

We have both local and overseas sourcing teams working closely with manufacturers that have a proven track record of making high-quality parts. As our global network provides virtually limitless capacity, your parts go directly into production at the most competitive prices.

After production, all parts are closely inspected twice*. Once at our manufacturing partners facilities and a second time at the Hubs quality control facility in either Chicago or Amsterdam. Within 24 hours, parts are cleared from customs and shipped from our facility to their final destination.

* All 3D printed parts are produced locally and only inspected at the manufacturing partner. These ship directly to their final destination.

Instant, online quotes

Instead of traditional, rules-based quoting, we use machine learning algorithms to compare your CAD to millions of previously manufactured parts and generate a quote instantly.

  • Upload a CAD to start (STEP, STP, IGES, IGS, SLDPRT, 3DM, SAT, STL, OBJ or X_T)

  • Price updates in real-time as you change materials, lead time, etc..

  • No hidden costs. Our instant quotes are not just indications, but the actual price, and include shipping and customs up front.

The Hubs Standard

Guaranteed quality, every time.

Threads and tolerances

Our manufacturing partners adhere to industry standards* for threads and tolerances.

* ISO 2768 (m/f) for metal parts, 2768-m for plastic parts, ISO and ANSI/ASME for threads.

Finishing & post-processing

Our manufacturing partners follow our strict technical requirements for finishing and post-processing.

Quality documentation

We provide quality documentation* such as First Article Inspection, Certificate of Conformance and Material Certificate/Test Report on request.

* For CNC machining and sheet metal fabrication.

Certification

Our quality management system is ISO 9001 certified and our manufacturing partners can provide additional certifications: AS 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 13485, IATF 16949.

Refunds and reorders

Are your parts not up to spec? Well reorder them for you for free, or provide a full refund.

Inspection process

All parts are closely inspected twice*; by a manufacturing partner and a Hubs quality control expert in Chicago or Amsterdam.

* All 3D printed parts are produced locally and inspected only at the manufacturing partner.

Manufacturing is about relationships, so we have staff on the ground wherever you are to help make sure everything runs smoothly and on time, every time.
